Cacti-nagios：cacti和nagios的整合版
2011-11-23 | pyinx | linux

Cacti-nagios is a self installing Linux Distribution based off CentOS that sets up and configures a customized Cacti and nagios install. Everything is designed to be completely automated and working directly out of the box. Both 32 Bit installations are possible from the same CD。
